SECTION 1: BUYER TYPE DETECTION
============================================================

TEST 1.1 — "I Know What I Want" Buyer
--------------------------------------
YOU SAY: "I need help with ESG compliance reporting for our
data center. What does that cost?"

EXPECTED:
- Alex should NOT push the free audit first
- Should ask 2-3 qualifying questions (facility size, current
  ESG tracking status, regulatory deadlines)
- Should give a pricing range ($5K-$15K)
- Should offer to generate a formal proposal
- Should ask for your name and email naturally

PASS CRITERIA:
[ ] Recognized you know what you want
[ ] Gave pricing range without forcing an audit
[ ] Asked qualifying questions
[ ] Attempted to capture contact info
[ ] Offered clear next step (proposal or call)

TEST 1.2 — "I Have a Problem" Buyer
--------------------------------------

EXPECTED:

- Should ask about facility size, rack count, PUE, cooling type
- Should frame the cost of inaction with specific numbers
- Should recommend Cooling Optimization service

PASS CRITERIA:
[ ] Asked diagnostic questions (SPIN method)
[ ] Referenced industry stats (40% energy cost, PUE benchmarks)
[ ] Quantified potential savings
[ ] Recommended a specific service
[ ] Offered the free review naturally
